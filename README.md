## Overview

Inventor Professional Suite is a complete mechanical engineering platform with advanced simulation, tube and pipe design, and tolerance analysis for professional product development.

Tube and pipe design creates routed piping and tubing systems within assemblies with automated routing, fitting placement, and bend calculations. The tube and pipe tools handle the specific requirements of hydraulic, pneumatic, and process piping design.

Tolerance analysis evaluates the cumulative effect of dimensional tolerances on assembly fit and function, identifying tolerance combinations that cause assembly problems before manufacturing. The analysis prevents the costly discovery of tolerance issues during production.

Nesting and flat pattern tools optimize material usage for sheet metal manufacturing with nesting algorithms that minimize waste and flat pattern generation that produces manufacturing-ready sheet metal blanks.

Inventor Professional Suite is the mechanical engineering platform for design teams that need comprehensive mechanical CAD with the simulation, specialized design tools, and documentation that professional product development demands.

## Performance & Integration

Inventor is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Inventor processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
